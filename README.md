# hyperliquid-trader-analysis
# Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment Analysis

## Objective

Analyze how market sentiment (Fear/Greed) impacts trader behavior and performance.

---

## Dataset

* Bitcoin Market Sentiment (Fear/Greed)
* Hyperliquid Trader Data

---

## Methodology

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Feature engineering (PnL, trade size, behavior)
* Sentiment-based analysis
* Trader segmentation

---

##Key Insights

* Higher profitability during Greed periods
* Traders take larger risks during Greed
* High trade size leads to higher returns but also higher losses

---

##Strategies

* Reduce risk during Fear
* Increase controlled exposure during Greed
* Avoid overtrading in volatile markets

---

## How to Run

1. Download dataset
2. Open `analysis.ipynb` in Jupyter/Colab
3. Run all cells

---
